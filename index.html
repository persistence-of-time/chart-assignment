Hate crimes have increased 350% in the NYPD 114th precinct

By: GUSTAVO GARCIA

Published: November 11, 2021

There has been an exponential increase in hate crime reports in the 114th police precinct of New York. The precinct covers Astoria, Long Island City, Woodside, and Jackson Heights, and is one of the most diverse areas of the city. Since the beginning of this year, there has been an increase of 350% of reported biased incidents in the area.

In total there have been nine incidents reported as of November, compared to two incidents in 2020.

“These are intense problems that come forward in moments of distress and [can] be brought forward by language of xenophobia” according to Arun Venugupal, senior reporter in race and justice at WYNC, during an interview with CBSN.

Out of the nine reported incidents only two have resulted in arrests in this precinct.
